About Hurst Plumbing Co.

Serving Mineola with Pride

Hurst Plumbing Co. has been serving the Mineola community and surrounding Wood County areas for years. As a locally-owned and operated plumbing company, we understand the unique needs of homeowners and businesses in our area. We're not just your plumbers - we're your neighbors.

Our commitment is simple: provide honest, reliable plumbing services at fair prices. We treat every home and business like it's our own, and we stand behind the quality of our work. When you call us, you're getting a team that cares about your satisfaction and your property.

What We Stand For

Local Expertise

We know the homes, the buildings, and the plumbing challenges specific to Mineola and East Texas. Our local knowledge means faster, more effective solutions.

Honest Service

We'll never recommend work you don't need. Our reputation in this community is built on honesty and transparency in everything we do.

Quality Workmanship

We use quality parts and proven techniques to ensure repairs last. Our work is done right the first time, backed by our satisfaction guarantee.

Customer First
